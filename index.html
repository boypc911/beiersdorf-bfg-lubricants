Company Profile

With two R&D and testing centers, three production bases and close cooperation with the State Key Laboratory for Internal Combustion Engines, the company focuses on developing and producing lubricants and car care products that meet customer needs.

North China Factory

The North China factory was put into operation in 2016. It consists of the comprehensive office area, testing and R&D center, production and blending area and raw material storage area.

Testing and Research Center

The center has more than sixty sets of advanced testing and laboratory equipment for physical and chemical indicator testing, helping improve product quality and competitiveness.

Production and Team

The 100,000-ton DCS automatic blending and filling equipment supports efficient, precise and environmentally friendly production. The company advocates honesty, friendliness, innovation, hard work and win-win cooperation.
