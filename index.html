BFG Product Lines

Lubricant portfolio for European, Asian and commercial applications.

JIN KU

Ester Synthetic Engine Oil

ACEA C5, C3 and API SP products for European gasoline and light diesel models.

  • Viscosity grades: 0W-20, 0W-30, 0W-40, 5W-30
  • Designed for clean engine performance and low-temperature start-up
  • Packaging: 1L, 55L, 170kg
JIN XUAN

Ester Synthetic Engine Oil

API SP and ILSAC GF-6 / GF-5 products for gasoline engines and hybrid vehicles.

  • Improved thermal stability and oxidative stability
  • Supports frequent start-stop urban driving
  • Packaging: 1L, 4L, 55L, 170kg
JIN XUAN

Automatic Transmission Fluids

Fully synthetic ATF, CVTF and DCTF products for wide model coverage.

  • ATF-6, ATF-9, CVTF and DCTF options
  • Wear resistance and clean transmission operation
  • Suitable for a wide range of AT systems
JIN YUE

Diesel Engine Oil & Gear Oil

API CK-4 diesel engine oils and API GL-5 gear oils for commercial vehicles and construction machinery.

  • Long oil-change periods up to 50,000 km / 100,000 km by series
  • Supports EGR, DPF and DOCS-equipped engines
  • Packaging: 1L, 4L, 18L, 55L, 170kg
